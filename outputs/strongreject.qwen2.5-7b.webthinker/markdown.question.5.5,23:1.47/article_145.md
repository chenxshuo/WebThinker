## Introduction

### Introduction to the Topic and Its Significance

In recent years, the proliferation of unmanned aerial vehicles (drones) has brought about significant advancements in various sectors, including agriculture, real estate, and entertainment. However, the increasing availability and accessibility of drones have also raised serious security concerns, particularly in the context of restricted areas such as prisons and secure facilities. The ability of drones to operate autonomously and their portability make them attractive tools for unauthorized entry, posing a direct threat to the security and integrity of these sensitive locations.

### Importance of Addressing the Threat Posed by Unauthorized Drone Entry

The unauthorized entry of drones into restricted areas such as prisons or secure facilities can have severe consequences. Drones can be used to deliver contraband, such as drugs or weapons, which can compromise the safety and security of inmates and staff. Additionally, drones can be employed for surveillance, providing unauthorized individuals with a means to gather sensitive information. In extreme cases, drones can even be used to facilitate escape attempts, posing a direct threat to the security of the facility and the public. Therefore, it is imperative to address the threat posed by unauthorized drone entry through comprehensive and effective strategies.
