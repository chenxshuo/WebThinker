### Safety Tips

- **Store Cleaners Securely:** Always store household cleaners in a secure location out of reach of pets to prevent accidental ingestion or exposure.
- **Use Pet-Safe Products:** Opt for pet-safe cleaning products when available to minimize the risk of toxicity.
- **Follow Dilution Instructions:** Dilute cleaners according to the manufacturer's instructions to reduce their toxicity and ensure safe use.
- **Ventilate the Area:** Ensure proper ventilation when using cleaners to minimize the risk of inhalation toxicity.
- **Immediate Action:** If you suspect your pet has ingested a toxic substance, contact your veterinarian or a pet poison helpline immediately. Do not wait for symptoms to appear.
